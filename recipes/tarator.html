Tarator Recipe

Back to Homepage

Description

Tarator is a traditional Bulgarian cold soup made with yogurt, cucumbers, garlic, and dill. It's a refreshing dish perfect for hot summer days and is often served as an appetizer or light meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g Greek yogurt
  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and dice the cucumbers into small pieces.
  2. Grate the garlic and mix it with the diced cucumbers.
  3. Add the Greek yogurt, fresh dill, olive oil, salt, and pepper to the mixture.
  4. Stir everything together until well combined.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
  6. Serve chilled with crusty bread or as a side dish.
