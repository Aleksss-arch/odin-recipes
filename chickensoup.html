Chicken Soup Recipe

Back to Homepage

Description

Chicken soup is a comforting and nutritious dish that's perfect for when you're feeling under the weather. This recipe is simple to make and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 cups chicken broth

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine the chicken pieces with the chicken broth.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, and bay leaf.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour.
  4. Remove the chicken pieces and set aside to cool.
  5. Strain the broth and discard the solids.
  6. Shred the cooled chicken meat and return it to the pot.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with crusty bread or as a side dish.
